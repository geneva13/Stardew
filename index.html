Stardew Guide

For nerds, by a nerd. Game on!

Daily Tasks Tips Products Gifts Skills

Daily Tasks

  • Check the TV (weather, fortune, and recipes)
  • Water crops (unless it’s raining or sprinklers are set)
  • Pet animals and harvest animal products
  • Collect artisan goods (cheese, wine, jam)
  • Clear debris/rocks/wood if needed
  • Visit villagers and give gifts (check birthdays!)
  • Check the calendar and quests on the board
  • Refill machines (preserves jar, kegs, furnaces)
  • Visit Pierre’s/shop/Clint/etc. before 5 PM
  • Forage around town and mines
  • Dump energy before bed—fishing, chopping, mining!

Quick Tips

  • Plan ahead for seasonal crops and tool upgrades
  • Save gold-quality crops for bundles or quests
  • Use staircases to speed through the Skull Cavern
  • Use rain to your advantage for fishing or upgrading

Best Products by Season (with Base Prices)

Spring

  • Strawberries (120g) – Buy during Egg Festival
  • Potatoes (80g) – Chance for multiple harvests
  • Catfish (200g) – Rainy days in rivers
  • Wild Horseradish (50g) – Forage

Summer

  • Blueberries (50g/each berry) – Multi-harvest king
  • Melons (250g) – High value, great for gifts too
  • Pike (100g) – Common in rivers
  • Spice Berry (80g) – Great forage find

Fall

  • Cranberries (75g/berry) – Profitable multi-yield
  • Pumpkins (320g) – Great for gifting and bundles
  • Salmon (75g) – River fish
  • Common Mushroom (40g) – Easy forage

Skill Bonuses by Level

Farming

  • +1 Watering Can & Hoe proficiency per level
  • Lvl 5: Rancher (+20% animal) or Tiller (+10% crops)
  • Lvl 10: Artisan goods worth 40% more (Artisan!)

Mining

  • +1 Pickaxe proficiency per level
  • Lvl 5: Miner (+1 ore per vein) or Geologist (gem chance)
  • Lvl 10: Excavator (double geodes!) or Prospector (coal)

Fishing

  • +1 Fishing Rod proficiency per level
  • Lvl 5: Fisher (+25% fish price) or Trapper (cheaper traps)
  • Lvl 10: Angler (+50% fish price!) or Pirate (double treasures)

Foraging

  • +1 Axe proficiency per level
  • Lvl 5: Forester (+25% wood) or Gatherer (+20% double forage)
  • Lvl 10: Botanist (always highest quality forage!)

Combat

  • +HP and weapon proficiency per level
  • Lvl 5: Fighter (+10% damage) or Scout (+critical strike chance)
  • Lvl 10: Brute (+15% damage) or Acrobat (special move cooldowns)
