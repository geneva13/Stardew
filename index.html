Quick Tips

  • Always check the TV every morning (weather, fortune, and recipes!).
  • Pet your animals and close the barn doors at night.
  • Plant crops in 3x3 for sprinklers later on.
  • Upgrade watering can just before rainy day.

Best Crops by Season

  • Spring: Strawberries, Cauliflower, Potatoes
  • Summer: Blueberries, Starfruit, Melons
  • Fall: Cranberries, Pumpkins, Sweet Gem Berries

Villager Gift Favorites

  • Abigail: Amethyst, Pumpkin
  • Leah: Salad, Goat Cheese
  • Linus: Cactus Fruit, Coconut
  • Sebastian: Frozen Tear, Sashimi

Tool Upgrade Order

  1. Copper Pickaxe
  2. Copper Axe
  3. Steel Pickaxe
  4. Steel Axe
  5. Upgrade Watering Can before rainy day

Controls Cheat Sheet (PC)

WASD: Move

Left Click: Use tool

Right Click: Check / interact

1-0: Toolbelt hotkeys

ESC: Open menu
